About Anise Restaurant and Wine Vault

With a carefully chosen array of artisanal wines and craft beers, The Anise Restaurant offers an ingredient-driven menu that captures the region's casual sophistication. Diners enjoy the indoor/outdoor setting, which features heritage olive trees, fountains, and sparkling chandeliers.

The iconic stone structure, which was constructed in 1904 and is listed on the National Register of Historic Places, has been transformed into a two-story wine vault. The wine vault, which has luxurious lounge seating, mammoth limestone bar, rough-cut fieldstone walls, 24-foot cedar ceiling, and rare, limited-production wines from Northern California's best small producers, is distinguished by these features. Take advantage of them by the glass, bottle or flight, or let our wine specialists assist you in building your own cellar.
